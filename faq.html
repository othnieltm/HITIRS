Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our internship program

General Questions

What is HIT Internship Recommendation System?
HIT IRS is a platform that connects HIT students with relevant internship opportunities based on their skills, interests, and academic performance.
Who can use this platform?
The platform is available to all current HIT students and registered partner companies offering internship positions.

Application Process

How do I create an account?
Use your HIT student email to register. Complete your profile with academic information, skills, and interests for better internship matches.
What documents do I need to apply?
You'll need your CV, academic transcript, and any relevant certificates. Some positions may require additional documentation.

Internship Details

How long are the internships?
Internships typically range from 3 to 6 months, depending on the company and position requirements.
Are internships paid?
This varies by company. Each listing will clearly indicate whether the position is paid, unpaid, or offers a stipend.
