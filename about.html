About HITIRS

Bridging the gap between talented students and industry-leading companies

Our Mission

At HITIRS, we're dedicated to empowering Harare Institute of Technology students with meaningful internship opportunities that launch successful careers. Our AI-driven platform creates perfect matches between student talents and industry needs.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform for connecting academic talent with industry opportunities in Zimbabwe and beyond, fostering innovation and professional growth.
